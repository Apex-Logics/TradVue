## General Best Practices

### Before Sending Any Outreach:

1. **Personalize.** Reference something specific about them—recent content, a quote, a mutual connection. Generic outreach gets ignored.

2. **Lead with value.** What's in it for *them*? Not what you want, what they get.

3. **Be clear on asks.** If you want an intro, say so. If you want a meeting, say so. Don't be vague.

4. **Make it easy to say yes.** Include next steps, timelines, and access (discount codes, trial access, etc.).

5. **Follow up, but respect boundaries.** 1–2 follow-ups are fine. 3+ is spam.

6. **Use the tools you have.** Founder story, metrics, testimonials—those are more convincing than flowery language.

---

### What to Track:

- **Response rate** (goal: 10–20% for cold outreach)
- **Meeting conversion** (emails → calls)
- **Deal conversion** (meetings → partnerships)
- **Time to close** (from first email to signed agreement)
- **ROI per partnership** (new users acquired, revenue, etc.)

---

### Pro Tips:

- **Best times to send:** Tuesday–Thursday, 10am–2pm (in their timezone if possible)
- **Email length:** 150–300 words is ideal. Respect their attention span.
- **CTA clarity:** "Reply with yes/no" works better than "let me know if you're interested"
- **Attachment strategy:** Attach 1–2 key documents max. Too many = ignored.
- **Tone:** Professional but conversational. You're a human, talk like one.

---

### Sample Discount/Affiliate Codes:

Use consistent naming: `[PARTNER_NAME_20]` for 20% discount codes, `AFF_[PARTNER_NAME]` for affiliate links.

Example: `FINNHUB_CASE20` for a data provider case study partnership with 20% discount.
