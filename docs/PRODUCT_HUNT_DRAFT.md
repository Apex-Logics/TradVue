## 3) Long Description

Most traders don’t actually review their performance — they just remember the big wins and painful losses.

TradVue is a trading journal built to help you improve your process, not just store trades.

With TradVue, you can:
- log and review trades in one place
- track performance across stocks, futures, forex, crypto, and options
- see your win rate, P&L, and setup-level patterns
- import or organize trades faster instead of fighting spreadsheets
- connect TradingView alerts/workflows to reduce manual entry

We built TradVue for traders who want structure, cleaner feedback loops, and better decisions over time.

If you’ve outgrown spreadsheets or generic note-taking tools, TradVue gives you a practical way to review what’s working, fix what isn’t, and trade with more consistency.

## 9) FAQ Draft for Comments

### What makes TradVue different?
TradVue is focused on **review and performance improvement**, not just storing trades. The goal is to help traders build a repeatable process.

### Who is it for?
Active traders, prop firm traders, discretionary traders, and anyone who has outgrown spreadsheets or inconsistent journaling.

### What markets does it support?
TradVue supports stocks, futures, forex, crypto, and options inside the journal workflow.

### Is this a broker?
No — TradVue is not a broker and does not execute trades. It’s a journaling, analytics, and review platform.

### Why launch now?
Because the core journaling and review workflow is already useful today, and we want product feedback from real traders as we keep improving the platform.
