# TradVue Positioning

## What We ARE
- **Trading Intelligence Platform**
- Market data aggregation & analysis
- Real-time news with AI sentiment
- Smart alerts & notifications
- Charts, watchlists, economic calendar
- Decision support for traders

## What We ARE NOT
- ❌ NOT a brokerage
- ❌ NOT a trading platform
- ❌ Users CANNOT buy/sell stocks/crypto/forex on TradVue
- ❌ No order execution
- ❌ No account balances or portfolios (of real money)

## How Users Trade
Users analyze on TradVue → Execute trades on their broker:
- Robinhood, TD Ameritrade, Schwab
- Interactive Brokers, Fidelity
- Coinbase, Binance (crypto)
- Their existing broker of choice

## Competitive Comparison
| Platform | What It Does |
|----------|-------------|
| **TradVue** | Intelligence & analysis (us) |
| TradingView | Charts & analysis |
| Finviz | Screening & analysis |
| Robinhood | Brokerage (trading) |
| TD Ameritrade | Brokerage (trading) |

## Future Consideration
- Potential broker API integration (read-only portfolio sync)
- Never order execution — always redirect to user's broker
