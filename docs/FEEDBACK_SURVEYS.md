# Feedback Survey Templates

## 1. NPS Survey (Net Promoter Score)

**Q1: How likely are you to recommend us to a friend or colleague?**
- Scale: 0 (Not likely) → 10 (Very likely)

**Q2: What's the main reason for your score?**
- Answer: Open text (short response)

**Q3: What's one thing we could improve?**
- Answer: Open text (short response)

---

## 2. Feature Feedback

**Q1: Which features do you use most?**
- Checkbox options: [Trading Dashboard], [Portfolio Analytics], [Alerts], [Mobile App], [API], [Other]

**Q2: How satisfied are you with overall platform usability?**
- Scale: 1 (Very unsatisfied) → 5 (Very satisfied)

**Q3: What feature would you like to see next?**
- Answer: Open text (short response)

**Q4: How responsive is our customer support?**
- Scale: 1 (Very slow) → 5 (Very fast)

**Q5: Any additional feedback?**
- Answer: Open text (short response)

---

## 3. Exit Survey

**Q1: Why are you leaving?**
- Options: [Switched to competitor], [Cost], [Features lacking], [Poor support], [Other reason]

**Q2: How likely would you return in the future?**
- Scale: 1 (Not likely) → 5 (Very likely)

**Q3: What could we have done better?**
- Answer: Open text (short response)

**Q4: Would you recommend us to others?**
- Options: [Yes], [No], [Maybe]

---

**Usage Notes:**
- Keep surveys under 2 minutes to complete
- Deploy after key actions (transaction, feature use, account closure)
- Review responses weekly for patterns
